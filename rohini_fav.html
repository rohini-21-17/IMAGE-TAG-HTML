JUST ROHINI THINGS

SOFIA THE FIRST

"Sofia the First" shows that true royalty comes from kindness, courage, and doing the right thing — not from wearing a crown.




HEY! SINAMIKA(DULQUEER)

Yaazhan shows that true love doesn’t control — it lets go, forgives, and stays kind, even when things don’t go your way.




SHINCHAN

Shinchan is a fearless, fun-loving kid who lives in the moment, makes everyone laugh, and doesn’t care what others think — and honestly,
I love how he reminds us to take life a little less seriously and enjoy the chaos.




SPONGE BOB

SpongeBob is an endlessly optimistic, goofy, and hardworking sea sponge who finds joy in the smallest things —
I admire how he teaches us to stay cheerful, kind, and true to ourselves, no matter how weird the world gets.




DORAEMON

Doraemon is a futuristic robot cat who helps Nobita with gadgets, but more importantly, teaches that shortcuts aren't always the solution —
I feel he’s like the friend we all wish we had: wise, caring, and always there when life gets tough.




PET OF SOFIA

In Sofia the First, Clover is a loyal, funny, talking rabbit who always supports Sofia with honest advice and comfort.
Though a bit lazy and snack-loving, his presence gives her courage, joy, and the feeling that she’s never alone —
having a pet like him would be like having a best friend for life.




MUSIC

Music is a true friend and pure emotion —
it soothes sadness, doubles happiness, speaks when words fail, and colors every moment of life with feeling.




DHANUSH

Dhanush, with countless awards and unstoppable growth, is my true inspiration —
his hard work shows that even if we fall at the first step, we must rise stronger.
His journey reminds me that talent shines brightest when fueled by persistence and humility.




LORD SHIVAN

Lord Shiva gives me a deep sense of peace, happiness, and a stress-free mind —
He’s my calm, my strength, and my happy place.




HEART BEAT

Heartbeat taught me that life is made of many stories —
each character, with their own struggle and strength, showed that every path is unique, emotional, and worth understanding.
